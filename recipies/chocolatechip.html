Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chocolate Chip is America's favorite cookie. The combination of vanilla and chocolate satisfies almost every sweet tooth.

Recipie

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups softened butter
  • 1¼ cups granulated sugar
  • 1¼ cups firmly packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 Tablespoon vanilla
  • 3¾ cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 12-ounce package semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350°F
  2. Combine butter, sugar, brown sugar, eggs and vanilla in bowl. Beat at medium speed until creamy. Add flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt; beat at low speed until well mixed. Stir in chocolate chips.
  3. Drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls, 2 inches apart, onto ungreased cookie sheets. Bake 10-12 minutes or until light golden brown. Cool 2 minutes on cookie sheet; remove to cooling rack.
